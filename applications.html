Why It Matters

Computational Thinking is not limited to computer science. It is used across industries to solve problems, improve efficiency, make predictions, and create innovative solutions for everyday challenges.

Major Applications

AI systems rely on algorithms, pattern recognition, and data analysis to learn and make decisions.

Computational models help doctors diagnose diseases, predict risks, and improve patient care.

Education

Personalized learning platforms use computational methods to improve educational experiences.

Environment

Data-driven approaches help manage resources, reduce waste, and support sustainability.

Smart Cities
